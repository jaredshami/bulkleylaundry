## Next Steps:

### Option 1: Quick Start (30 minutes)
1. Go to **unsplash.com**
2. Search "laundry services"
3. Download 5-6 high-quality images
4. Drag them into your `/images/` folders
5. Done! Site displays them automatically

### Option 2: Professional (1-2 hours)
1. Use **Canva** to design custom service graphics
2. Generate images with **AI tool** (Midjourney/DALL-E)
3. Compress with **TinyPNG**
4. Upload to folders
5. Site auto-loads them

### Option 3: DIY (flexible timeline)
1. Take your own photos of:
   - Business location
   - Equipment
   - Team members
   - Sample laundry
2. Compress with **Squoosh.app**
3. Upload to folders
