Frequently Asked Questions

How is laundry weight calculated?

Laundry is weighed clean and dry. Most loads range from 3-7 lbs. A typical family's weekly wash is about 10-15 lbs.

Do you have rush services?

Yes! Add 25% to any service for 24-hour express turnaround. Contact us to confirm availability.

What about delicate items?

Delicate fabrics are handled separately and can be hand-washed for premium care. Ask about our delicate wash service.

Are pickups really free?

Yes, free pickup and delivery within 5 miles of our location. Orders over $50 include free service anywhere in our area.
